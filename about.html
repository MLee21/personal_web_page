About

Hi, I'm Minnie Lee! I was born in Seoul, South Korea, but I grew up in Denver, and I love it!
This site was created to give you a taste of Denver and its rich culture through my eyes.
I am a heavy advocate of women doing what they love and being empowered by their passions.
I would like to expound upon the introduction of Asians into media with shows like "Fresh Off the Boat"
and Youtube famous makeup artists, but mostly, I just want to give back to my community.
I am classically trained on the piano, read like a fiend, take hip hop dance,
and I am currently learning how to program.
I am heavily involved in the Denver music scene
and was interning at Dryer Plug Studios.
So, while you're listening to the featured local band after your wonderful session at the Block 1750,
and receiving your daily dose of inspiration from the daily quotes, take in Denver!
It's a pretty rad place. Cheers!
